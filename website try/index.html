About Us

We are a group of 3rd Year Computer Engineering students from the University of Batangas Lipa Campus. Our sole objective is to improve the traditional style of websites in terms of online shopping, specifically those from clothing lines.

  • ✔️ Our website aims to be more user friendly. The clothing selections auto-sort itself according to the user's style and needs.
  • ✔️ Easier to access, even kids can do it.
  • ✔️ Integration of management interface and ordering system within the website.

Our ultimate goal is to improve user experience and deliver a more efficient way of online shopping, making it more fun!

Services

We offer a platform for buying, managing, and organizing clothing orders online. Admins can manage inventory and users can easily find what suits their style.
